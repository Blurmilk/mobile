PROJECT

NEED NO DEFINITION

What is standard?
people often define luxury as fashion and make it as industrial standard. Luxury has taken away the public's right to talk about fashion. It becomes an authority, something that cannot be questioned. But is it really so? what is beauty? what is fashion? what is the definition of fashion?
Street atmosphere of two sides of the site formed completely different contrast. I put this contrast embodied in my design.The main part of the design is a high-grade fashion shopping center, and it is surrounded by an open-air night market. These two parts formed a contrast in form, which not only responses to the condition of the site, but also expresses the doubt to the "Fashion".

PRESERVING MEMORIES

This project is designed for aboriginal habitants,whose production and reproduction are combined. It offers residents different types of family space and shop space and make the combination of them to keep the characteristic of combining production space and reproduction space. The frames added on facades allow the residents to design freely, which will maintain the spontaneity and continue the historical context.

BETWEEN IN AND OUT

What is community?
Tulou is a kind of defensive architecture in China, which is closed to the outside and open to the inside. This design hopes to open this closed building so that it can open outward while retaining the its original community characteristics, and rebuild a new social structure.

BECOMING MASTERS

Anthropocentrism is the belief that people are the most important thing in the world. this attitude strongly affects domestic pets. When we are providing shelters for them, we unavoidably endow them with human qualities, making their houses cater to human aesthetics and lifestyle even if we set their needs as priorities. But is this really reasonable for animals? Must our space be human-centered? This project focused on the most common household pets——cats, to imagine a non-anthropocentric architecture, and to reflect how to deal with the relationship between human, animals and environment.
